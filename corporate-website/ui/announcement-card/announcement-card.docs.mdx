---
description: An announcement card component for displaying news or updates.
labels: ['card', 'announcement', 'news']
---

The `AnnouncementCard` component is designed to showcase news articles or press releases with a title, summary, formatted date, and a link to the full content.

## Basic Announcement

This example demonstrates a basic announcement card with a title, summary, date, and link.

```jsx
<AnnouncementCard
  title="Mars Habitation Module Alpha: Online!"
  summary="Corporate Inc. proudly announces the successful deployment and activation of the first sustainable habitat module on the Martian surface. This marks a significant milestone in our mission to populate Mars."
  date="2042-10-28T10:00:00Z"
  linkUrl="/news/mars-habitat-alpha-online"
  linkText="Discover More"
/>
```
